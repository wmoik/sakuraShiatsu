Shiatsu. Acupuncture delivered through finger pressure.

Shiatsu is a traditional Oriental message therapy technique that utilizes pressure points to increase energy flow through Meridian Flow Pathways. These techniques are meant to break apart the blocks that we build through our repititive daily actions and tense muscles. These techniques are similar to that of acupuncture, which target the meridian point with needles instead of finger pressure.

Deep Tissue. Western-style muscular therapy

Deep tissue massage targets the muscles directly by applying strong but slow massage strokes over musculature.

Hot Stone Massage. Increased blood flow.

The hot stones used in this technique allow for blood vessel dilation allowing for greater blood flow to your skin and muscles while receiving a full massage. The heat from the stones also provide an additional relaxation element.
